Planning

Introduction

This game is about a guy wanting to wake up and wanting to play video games, so he goes over to his console and turns it on and ends up getting put into the game itself a bit like Jumanji

Game Hook

The Hook for the game is Bounce pads the player must use them to traverse through the level to collect what is needed to complete the level and finish the game the player must use them correctly or it could result in hurting the player.

General Information

The Target audience for my game would be anybody who likes playing and speedrunning 2d platformers as they would be able to time how quick they complete the game and be able to enjoy running and jumping around the level

Story and Character

The Story for this game is that a boy wakes up and wants to play a video game but when he turns on his console, he ends up getting sucked into the game and he has no clue what is going on, all that he knows is that he must complete the game to escape.

Gameplay

The Player must collect coins to complete the level and then the player must fight a boss without being killed and the only way to beat the boss is by collecting cursed coins to deal damage to the boss also the player must use mushrooms to bounce around the levels to collect coins and dodge spikes thats in the players way.

Mechanics

The Players mechanics are that the player can run and jump around the level and jump on mushrooms to launch themselves around the level to survive the player also can interact with chests, levers and consoles to activate parts of the game.

Level Design

The First Level design is a bit like a maze the player must travel round the level and collect enough coins to complete it without taking damage and killing the player where the second level design is more of a boss fight arena with some platforms to jump on to dodge and collect coins to destroy the boss.

Game Contols

The player has multiple different controls that allow them to travel around the levels to be able to collect coins to be able to beat the game.

Input Action
D Moves Right
A Moves Left
Space Jump
Hold Shift Sprints
E Interact with Objects
Overlap Objects Collects collectables or enables interaction
